      Saving your places

      Nothing is saved on a server. Two easy ways to keep these:

      1. Take a screenshot. On most phones: press the side button + volume-up at the same time.
      2. Send the page to yourself on WhatsApp. Tap your browser's share button, then "WhatsApp", then send to your own number.
